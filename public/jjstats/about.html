About Us

A site dedicated to analyzing submissions in no-gi Jiu Jitsu.

Who We Are

We love facts. We love statistics. We love Jiu Jitsu. We're nerds. With that being said, aggregating and analyzing data poses many challenges.

Accuracy of Data

Currently, there is no central body that collects submission data. Data is pulled from various sources as well as analyzing tournament footage.

We're only human. Data will have some margin of inaccuracies. Jiu Jitsu isn't always black and white. How is an arm lock different than an armbar? Was that a D'arce or an Anaconda?

As the sport evolves, submissions will evolve, positions evolve, and semantics evolve. We do our best to represent data as unbiased as possible.

Disclaimer

This site is for entertainment purposes only. So don't come after us. However, if you do notice a discrepancy, please feel free to shoot us a message.
